Chicken Pilau

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of rice
  • 1/2 kg chicken
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Spices (pilau masala, cumin, black pepper)
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Fry onions until golden brown.
  2. Add chicken and cook until browned.
  3. Add spices and ginger-garlic paste.
  4. Add rice and water (1:2 ratio), cover and cook.

Nutritional Info: High in protein and carbs, perfect for energy!
