Ingredients

  • 1/2 kg beef, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 1 onion
  • Salt and spices
  • 2 cups maize flour
  • Water

Instructions

  1. Cook beef with onions and tomatoes until tender.
  2. Add spices and simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Boil water and add maize flour slowly while stirring.
  4. Keep stirring until thickened. Serve hot.
